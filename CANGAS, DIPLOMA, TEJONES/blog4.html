Ilaya Mountain Resort


Inhale fresh air and exhale stress as you unwind in this mountain resort.

A stay of total relaxation awaits you at Ilaya Highland Resort. Located in the hills of Barangay Patag, Silay City, in Negros Occidental, the resort provides a serene and silent retreat.There is no shortage of green in these resort grounds, and every angle is simply relaxing. For the eyes of a weary city-dweller, the surrounding greenery is surely a welcome sight that brings waves of relaxation.
