Carbin Reef


The Carbin Reef is a spotless white sandbar that sits off the shore of Sagay town in the province of Negros Occidental. Aside from its beautiful white sand, the reef's surrounding waters are teeming with wildlife so make sure to bring some snorkeling gear during your visit here. Underneath the waves, you will find a vibrant aquatic ecosystem filled with colorful schools of fish and other marine animals.
