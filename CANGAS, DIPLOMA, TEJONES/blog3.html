GUINTUBDAN PAVILION


If you are a nature lover, want to meditate or just want to relax and want to breathe fresh air. Just visit Guintubdan Mt. Resort is the right one for you to visit. This place plunge coolness of the spring water and falls, and the awesome sights that you will view. This place is wonderful and perfect to relax.

From Bacolod City to La Carlota City it takes more than 30 minutes riding a bus, then from La Carlota market ride a PUJ to Guintubdan Mt. Resorts takes more than 18-20 minutes. There were no entrance fee and you can inquire room’s rates inside.

You will feel quietness of the place and you can breathe fresh air (stress free). It’s perfect for mountaineering, well for health and you can also exercise because you will enjoy to take the stairs up and down like 60-70 steps just to see and swim on their twin falls below the resort.

We recommend to bring your own food. It’s is one of the coldest place in La Carlota City and experience the Fog.
