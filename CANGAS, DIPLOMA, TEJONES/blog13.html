Blue Waves Resort Sipaway Island


Located in the island of Sipaway that is 15-20 minutes of boat ride from the port. This place is truly relaxing and magical. If you are looking for a budget-friendly resort that has a lot of activities, then this place is perfect. The resort also offers free Wi-fi all throughout its place. This place is currently rising to the top as one of the best beach resort in the city. From the crystal clear waters and white sands, this place is truly one of a kind that you will surely miss.
